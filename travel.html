Places I’ve visited

I have travelled to many interesting places around the globe. I have travelled to all 6 inhabited continents. Some of my favourite destinations are London, England and Rome. Italy

London, England

I visted London, England in 2012 to watch the Olympics and Paralympics. During the Olympics, we went to watch many sports including rowing and the marathon. Between the Olympic and Paralympics, we did a lot of sightseeing in London and the surrounding area.

Rome, Italy

I visited Rome, Italy this past summer. We spent 10 days in Rome, 4 days in Florence, and 6 days in Venice. The trip was really fun and one of the highlights of the trip was the colosseum in Rome.
