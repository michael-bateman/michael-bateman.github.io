Places I’ve Visited

I have visited many interesting places around the globe by travelling. I’ve travelled to all 6 inhabited continents. Some of my favourite travel destinations are London, England, and Rome, Italy.

London, England

I visited London, England in 2012 in time for the London 2012 Summer Olympics. We went to many great sporting events during the Olympics and stayed 5 days after the Olympics ended. The reason we decided to stay later was because if we stayed, there would be less line-ups for activities such as the London Eye. At the end of the trip, the 5 extra days were awesome and during that time we got to do the most of our sightseeing.

Rome, Italy

I visited Rome, Italy this past summer and it was a lot of fun. We visited the colosseum which was really cool. Yeah that’s all I have to say…
