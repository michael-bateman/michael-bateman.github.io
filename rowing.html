Rowing

Rowing is a fun sport that requires a lot of commitment. I row at the Argonaut Rowing Club, the Hanlan Boat Club, and at my high school, Upper Canada College. This year, I am in the JV program at UCC. This past summer, I competed at the Royal Canadian Henley Regatta in Welland Ontario, which is one of the biggest club - sprint races.
