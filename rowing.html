Rowing


About

Rowing is a fun sport that requires a lot of early morning commitment. I row at my high school, Upper Canada College in the spring, Argonaut Rowing Club in the summer and Hanlan Boat Club in the fall. This year, I was part of the JV program at UCC rowing in the 66kg 4+ and 72kg 8+. Last summer, I competed at the Royal Canadian Henley Regatta in St. Catherines Ontario with Argonaut racing in the 64kg 4+.
